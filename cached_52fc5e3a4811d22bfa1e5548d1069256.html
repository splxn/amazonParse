5.0 out of 5 starsGreat replacement for my T430
By bmb5 on February 2, 2016
Verified Purchase
This was just what i needed to breath some life back into my older Lenovo Thinkpad T430. The battery had been degrading over the past year and really was just about useless without a charger around. This battery fit perfectly and I had a laptop that could be used on my lap once again with no fuss whatsoever.

I took the liberty to peel back the sticker and found six LG 18650 cells just like the manufacturer specified in their listing. The voltages were all well balanced and an ideal 3.9V for storing and shipping. 3.9V is right around 70% full, so if you needed to pop this in and run you probably could get away with it. A safe bet is obviously to charge immediately after installation the first time. I have yet to run some careful capacity tracking, but will try to get around to that shortly. My first impression after these past few days is that the battery works about as well as the stock battery it came with. I would like batteries now could do better (not just the same), but this is not nearly as expensive as some of the other replacement options out there.

This will do the trick for me, sure beats buying a new laptop. I received this product at a discount and in return am providing my honest unbiased review.
