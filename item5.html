3.0 out of 5 starsNot as bright as I had hoped and expected
By Rjmurbil on July 29, 2014
Color: Black Verified Purchase
Disappointed , not that bright.
This desk lamp is actually quite nice with fast shipping from seller " Sunvalleytek " , good build quality, and very good light diffusion ; however, it is not very bright on its highest setting.
There are plenty of glowing 5 star reviews on Amazon for this specific lamp ( a significant number of which are not " verified purchase " reviews ) and for the most part I am in agreement in regards to the lamp's overall quality. It is just not that bright, as it turns out the lamp's rated 450 lumens is actually equivalent to a 40 watt incandescent bulb, again, not that bright.
My plan was to use this lamp as a replacement for an old spring loaded articulated arm desk lamp that finally wore out. I had used that lamp for illuminating a tabletop workspace while working on small hobbyist electronic projects ( assembling small PC boards with small electronic components and soldering ). The light diffusion of the TaoTronics lamp is really great for minimizing light shadowing and ghosting, easy on the eyes, but the lamp's output is simply not bright enough for my needs.
I honestly believe there is nothing misleading in this lamp's product description; however, the reviews ( again, majority not "verified purchase " ) did lead me to believe that the lamp would be brighter than I have experienced. Integrity aside, some people got something for something.
TaoTronics, if you built a lamp with the same overall build quality as this one but with greater output ( brightness ), I would not hesitate to purchase it.
As it stands I'm vacillating between return and keep with this lamp. It is a little pricey and I can't use it for my intended purpose but it is a well made lamp.

5.0 out of 5 starsFunctional, Fashionable, Really Nice Desk Lamp...
By JP TOP 500 REVIEWER on May 29, 2014
Color: Black
Length: 2:28 Mins
Video Review Summary:
The TaoTronics TT-DL05 Dimmable LED Desk Lamp is a great lamp for desktop use, it might even be great for crafters. Nice soft light that is easy on the eyes, and great for detailed work projects.

The design of this lamp has the light on a extension rod that can be adjusted to where you need it. The lighting rod can rotate up and down, side to side and be adjusted at an angle on the side.

Setup is easy, attach the power cable to the unit, then plug the it in. The level of light can be adjusted up to 7 different levels. The light is turned on by pressing the power button on the far left then moving your finger to the right until you find your desired light level. To adjust the light after turning it on, slide your finger left or right until you reach your desired light level. 7 red LEDS let you know what light level you are currently on. A cool feature about this light is after you turn it off, when you turn it back on it will automatically remember the last level you used.

Overall, the TaoTronics LED Desk Lamp is a well-built product that is very modern looking. Currently available in black or pink to fit your preferred style choices. The lamp performs as expected and would look and work great in an office or craft room.
