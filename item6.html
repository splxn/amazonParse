5.0 out of 5 starsGreat speaker, even better battery
By R. Taylor on March 6, 2015
Verified Purchase
This thing is great. I take it in the shower with me so I can jam out while charging my phone in the other room. Although it's highly water resistant, I still keep it on a shower rack and not within the water spray. I haven't tested the IP66 rating and don't really want to find out how wet it needs to get before it stops working.
The controls on the top of the speaker work well with minimal delay when switching between tracks. I've also taken a few calls through the speaker and have had no issues with it.
It feels durable and the power switch, auxiliary port, and charging port are underneath a rubber cap which slips on tightly. I attempted to duplicate the paracord holster for it but never completed it however I like how it protects the rubber from scrapes.

What really prompted me to write this review near midnight is the fact that THE BATTERY NEVER DIES. I purchased this speaker mid November and use it a few hours a day several days a week.

----I only charged it when I took it out of the box and tonight, March 6, 2015 at 11:52pm the battery died.----

Worth every penny.

The Photive Hydra Bluetooth 4.0 Speaker is a small and loud Bluetooth speaker that has a unique feature.

Box Contents (Thick cardboard box):
-Photive Hydra Bluetooth Speaker
-Micro USB cable
-Aux cable
-Instruction manual

Pros:
-Quality sound (2x 3.5 watt drivers) (15w output) (highs are clear and mids sound good)
-Easy to set up
-Small and portable
-Controls like play/pause, change volume and change song
-Charges by micro USB; I do not like mini USB or unique/old plugs people don't use today
-Silicon cover all over the speaker give the speaker good grip when carrying
-Rugged design and cool looking
-On/off switch
-Water/weather proof and dust proof/shockproof (submergible) (IP66 rated)
-Aux port for playing non Bluetooth devices
-Long battery life on 1 charge (8 hours)
-Built in microphone
-Great gift for music lovers like me
-Great price for a waterproof speaker!

Cons:
-Silicon piece surrounding the speaker makes it hard to shake all the water off if no towel is available
-Bass is not too good but sound quality overall is acceptable

Overall, this is great choice if you are looking for a waterproof and great sounding Bluetooth speaker that is small and looks good.
