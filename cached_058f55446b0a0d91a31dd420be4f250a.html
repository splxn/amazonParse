5.0 out of 5 starsA must have accessory to go with ThinkPad 10.
By A. WU on November 4, 2014
Verified Purchase
When I first purchased ThinkPad 10, I thought I could get away with buying this ultrabook keyboard, since I already have a bluetooth mouse (Microsoft Wedge Mouse) and keyboard (Apple Keyboard). However, my normal work usage pattern (i.e. programming + browsers, excel spreadsheets open etc) show that key may get stuck on my bluetooth keyboard, meaning that same character is being spit out on the screen repetitively.

Perhaps, it is because ThinkPad 10 gets too busy, so the bluetooth gets messed up. At the end of the day, I just could not work with bluetooth keyboard with ThinkPad 10.

After some debate, I ended up getting the Ultrabook keyboard to go with the ThinkPad 10. Although you do pay extra 100 for this accessory, you do end up getting a great ThinkPad keyboard typing experience, and my previously key-stuck issue is no longer a problem, as bluetooth is not involved here.

Personally, I'd say that if you intend to keep your ThinkPad 10, you are much better off getting this keyboard. However, at this pricing point, you may be better off with a Surface Pro 3.

Just a note that the keyboard does not come with the red nipple, so it may be a deal breaker for some of you loyal ThinkPad fans out there.
