4.0 out of 5 starsGreat Value- So Far So Good
By NellB on August 25, 2010
Verified Purchase
I was hesitant to purchase this item since at the time of purchase there were no reviews on Amazon to rate it. Lenovo (previously IBM) is a solid brand and I figured that this purchase would not disappoint. It hasn't so far. Don't expect lightning fast speed, but overall this all-in-one was a great buy. I use it for online classes, so I don't expect to be loading up my computer with large files or large amounts of music or downloading. It came with a keyboard and mouse- both work great. The built-in speakers are a little disappointing, but certainly do their job. My laptop is my main computer, so as a supplementary pc, this does its job.

The only thing I didn't like about this pc was that it came pre-loaded with Microsoft XP. XP is so outdated, so I'm not sure why they are selling computers still with this OS. I purchased Microsoft Office and while on the phone with Microsoft, they offered me an upgrade to Windows 7 for a minimal fee. I did that. Only problem is that you can not do an upgrade from XP to 7, only Vista to 7. I spent an hour and a half on the phone with tech support while they showed me how to do a clean download of Windows 7. Once I did that, everything is even better. I am happy with this purchase, and the value can't be beat.
