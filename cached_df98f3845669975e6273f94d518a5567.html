2.0 out of 5 starsIt may be an i7 processor but it's only dual-core. Quite Disappointed
By H. Reed on February 1, 2016
Verified Purchase
I just received this laptop today so I can only review what I've found right off the bat. First thing I noticed is that it takes 3 - 5 minutes just to boot up. It was loaded with bloatware that I immediately uninstalled. The keyboard is not backlit (my fault, I read the description wrong). But the one thing that really disappoints is the quality (or lack thereof) of the speakers. They're absolutely horrible. There's so much static that that is all you hear. I bought this laptop because of the 'High Performance' rating for multimedia usage. I record/edit videos for online streaming wildlife cams and the audio is so bad that all you hear is static.

03-07-16 UPDATE: I wish I had paid more attention to the specs and less attention to the glowing reviews. Keep in mind - this is a dual-core processor, low end but expensive. The 'High Performance' description is deceptive. You can't get high performance from a dual-core processor. A quad-core is necessary for heavy loads. This laptop can't even handle screen recording software. All the recordings drop frames/lag terribly. It's like watching a series of still shots rather than video. Very disappointed in this purchase but even more disappointed in myself for not paying more attention to the specs,
