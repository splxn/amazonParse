4.0 out of 5 starsGood laptop
By jade on March 7, 2016
Verified Purchase
It's a good laptop with the ability to be great. It has Bluetooth ability, it connects to Wi-Fi both rooted and mobile. The sound is average. This is my business laptop, I am in stocks and foreign exchange so knowing Lenovo to be business oriented is why I went with this brand. The keyboard took me some getting used to because some of the keys are smaller than the standard keyboard. It's all good now though. It's got the ability to be a great laptop bc you have easy access to the memory and hard drive, mine came with 4g of ram and a 500gb hard drive. I plan on adding 4 more gigs of ram and a 250 gb ssd which is supposed to be faster. Great price for a good laptop. No back-lit keyboard though, and the keys are chicklet style which I don't really care for but wasn't a deal breaker.
